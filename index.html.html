About our work

Why we exist

In conflict-affected regions, children often lose homes, schools and caregivers. Our local teams provide child protection, temporary shelter, safe water and education so children can heal and families can recover. We partner with community leaders to ensure long-term, sustainable solutions and to protect the most vulnerable.

Your donation helps buy emergency kits, train community carers and reopen classrooms every gift matters.

Volunteer or partner with us

We work with trained local staff. If you have relevant experience education, logistics, healthcare. register and we'll be in touch.
